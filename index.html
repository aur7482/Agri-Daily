Maize (Zea mays)

Staple Crop

Cereal Annual

Optimal Conditions

  • Temperature: 18-32°C
  • Rainfall: 500-800mm per cycle
  • Altitude: 0-2500m above sea level
  • Soil: Well-drained loamy soils

Growth Timeline

  • Planting to Germination: 5-7 days
  • Vegetative Stage: 30-40 days
  • Flowering: 50-65 days
  • Maturity: 90-120 days

Common Varieties

DH02 PH4 SC513 H614 KH500

Common Pests and Diseases

Maize Stem Borer

Larvae bore into stems causing wilting and reduced yield.

Damage: 20-70% yield loss Control Methods

Maize Lethal Necrosis

Viral disease causing yellowing, stunting and plant death.

Spread: Virus, vectors Control Methods

Fall Armyworm

Invasive pest that feeds on leaves and reproductive parts.

Damage: Up to 100% loss Control Methods

Grey Leaf Spot

Fungal disease causing rectangular lesions on leaves.

Favored by: Humid conditions Control Methods
